How It Works

Step 1: Upload Your Document

Simply upload your study materials in PDF or DOCX format. This could be anything from lecture notes and textbook chapters to research papers and articles.

Step 2: AI-Powered Content Generation

Our intelligent algorithms analyze your document and automatically generate a concise summary, a set of flashcards for key terms and concepts, and a multiple-choice quiz to test your knowledge.

Step 3: Study and Master

Use the generated materials to study smarter, not harder. Review the summary for a quick overview, test your memory with the flashcards, and assess your understanding with the quiz. You can save your study sets to your dashboard for easy access anytime.
